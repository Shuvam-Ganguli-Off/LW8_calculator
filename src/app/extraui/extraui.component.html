Calculator Tool

This is a calculator app with some very basic functions, such as:

  • Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication and Division on two numbers
  • Finding the Factorial of a number - The factorial of a number is the function that multiplies the number by every natural number below it.
  • Finding if a number is prime or composite - Prime numbers are the numbers that have only two factors, that are, 1 and the number itself. While, composite numbers are numbers with more than two factors.
